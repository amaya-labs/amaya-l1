
# Legal and Project Status

Amaya L1 is an independent research and development initiative exploring a permissioned Avalanche Layer 1 network.

## Current Status

- Amaya L1 is currently in planning, documentation, and proof-of-concept development.
- No public mainnet is represented by this repository.
- No production payment system is currently operated.
- No government deployment or approval is implied.
- No financial institution, remittance provider, transport operator, or enterprise partnership is implied unless formally announced.
- No token sale, investment offer, or fundraising offer is made through this repository.

## Testnet Assets

`TAMAYA` is a valueless testnet asset intended only for:

- Testing transaction fees
- Wallet testing
- Smart-contract testing
- Validator and RPC testing
- Application integration testing

TAMAYA is not intended for sale, investment, exchange listing, or financial gain.

## AMAYA Asset

Any future production AMAYA asset would require separate:

- Technical design
- Legal review
- Smart-contract audit
- Token-allocation policy
- Vesting policy
- Treasury controls
- Mainnet migration policy
- Regulatory assessment

Nothing in this repository guarantees that AMAYA will be launched, traded, listed, or have monetary value.

## Government and Institutional Concepts

Government payment proofs, document verification, remittance settlement, and institutional applications described in this repository are research concepts only.

Demonstrations must use synthetic or mock data unless an authorized agreement permits real data.

Any government demonstration must clearly state:

> Prototype using synthetic data. No government affiliation, official record, or real public payment is represented.

## Privacy

Personal, financial, health, location, government-identification, and confidential institutional information should not be stored directly on-chain.

Amaya L1 is intended to record only necessary proofs, hashes, timestamps, status records, and authorized transaction data.
